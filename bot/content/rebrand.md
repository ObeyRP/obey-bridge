**Same city. New name. Sharper edges.**

After a long run as **BritLife**, we're stepping into the next chapter
as **Obey RP** — same servers, same staff, same characters, fresh
identity.

## What's changing

- **Brand & website** — we're now at **[obeyrp.uk](https://obeyrp.uk)**.
  Your old bookmarks for britlifereality.co.uk will redirect.
- **Discord** — *this* server, renamed. You don't need to do anything;
  your roles, ranks, and history carry over.
- **Forum** — the portal now has a built-in forum at
  [obeyrp.uk/forum](https://obeyrp.uk/forum) — suggestions, staff
  announcements, and replies live there. The Discord stays your
  primary place to talk; the forum is the long-form record.
- **Plus** — `Obey Plus` replaces the old donator package. Existing
  subscribers keep their perks; the storefront now lives at
  **obeyrp.tebex.io** (any old britlifereality.tebex.io bookmarks
  redirect there).
- **Coins** — the in-game currency formerly known as "BritCoins" is
  now **Obey Coins**. Balances unchanged.

## What's NOT changing

- **Your character.** Your citizenid, your money, your job, your house,
  your phone, your friends list — same database, same world.
- **The rules.** Existing FearRP and NLR carry over. Read them at
  [obeyrp.uk/rules](https://obeyrp.uk/rules).
- **The staff team.** Same people. Same DMs open.
- **The server.** Connect via the new portal:
  [obeyrp.uk](https://obeyrp.uk) → **Connect to Server.**

## What we'd like you to do

1. Bookmark **[obeyrp.uk](https://obeyrp.uk)**.
2. Have a look around — `/leaderboards`, `/wiki`, `/forum`,
   `/dashboard` for the member view.
3. If you have feedback on the rebrand or the new site, drop it in
   `#suggestions` or the portal forum — both feed the same place.

Thanks for sticking with us. Same city. New name.
